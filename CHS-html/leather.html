Leather

The process of creating leather involves several steps. First, the raw animal hide or skin is obtained, typically from cows, sheep, goats, or pigs. This raw material undergoes a process called tanning, which stabilizes the proteins and prevents decay. Tanning can be done using different methods, with the most common ones being vegetable tanning and chrome tanning.
