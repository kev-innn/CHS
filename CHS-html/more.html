About Us

Welcome to CHS International. With 30 years of experience and a deep understanding of the global leather market, we are dedicated to providing our clients with top-quality leather products and a seamless business experience.

At CHS International, we specialize in sourcing a wide range of premium leather materials. We have built strong relationships with reputable tanneries and suppliers, allowing us to access a diverse selection of leather types, grades, and finishes. From full-grain to suede, from vegetable-tanned to chrome-tanned, we offer a comprehensive range of options to cater to the unique needs of our clients.

Quality is our utmost priority. We meticulously select each piece of leather, conducting thorough inspections to ensure that it meets our stringent quality standards. We understand that our clients rely on us to deliver materials that are not only visually appealing but also durable and consistent. By maintaining strict quality control measures, we strive to build long-term relationships based on trust and reliability.

At CHS International, we value professionalism and integrity. We approach every transaction with honesty, transparency, and a commitment to fair business practices.We believe in forging strong partnerships and aim to be a trusted and reliable partner for all your leather requirements.

Thank you for considering CHS International as your preferred partner in the leather market. We look forward to serving you with our extensive range of high-quality leather, industry expertise, and exceptional customer service. Let us help you navigate the world of leather and provide you with the materials you need to bring your vision to life.
